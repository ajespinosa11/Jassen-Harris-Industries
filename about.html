About Jassen Harris Industries Corporation


Jassen Harris Industries Corporation, also known as Makerlab, was founded in the year 2016, driven be a team of passionate electronics experts who understand the challenges that the community is facing. The company started by selling electronics on buy-and-sell sites or meeting with buyers personally. In 2016, this is where they finally established Makerlab which became a one-stop destination for all things related to DIY electronics. All hobbyists, students and professionals were catered to, providing high-quality products but with afffordable options.

"We want to tell them, don't be afraid. Even if problems arise, the replacement parts can be delivered quickly and won't break the bank"

Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and innovative products that unlock the full potential of Filipinos through robotics, the Internet of Things (IoT), electronics, and advanced manufacturing.



Vision

To be the premier destination in the Philippines for robotics, electronics, and advanced manufacturing needs - every maker's choice.
